About ADS Mobile Services

Your trusted partner for professional mobile auto repair services

Our Story

Founded in 2016, ADS Mobile Services was born from a simple idea: auto repair should be convenient, reliable, and honest. After years of working for Mercedez-Benz dealerships, the founder Andrew Wilkinson recognized that customers needed a better way to get their vehicles serviced without the hassle of scheduling around shop hours, arranging transportation, or waiting in uncomfortable waiting rooms.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of customers across the metro area. Andrew is a certified technician who brings quality service and integrity to every job. Providing you with a personalized convenient experience.
